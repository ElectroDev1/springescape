Play the latest demo - Spring Escape: Sneak Peek

Spring Escape: Sneak Peek is the new demo featuring a ton of changes and improvements both visually, technically, and mechanically for Spring Escape, a charming 2D platformer about a not-so-unlikely duo making their ways and digging through snazzy environments filled with obstacles.

  • Revamped nearly every graphic in the game

  • A brand new version of the first level with a little cutscene to boot and a new completion condition

  • New movement options to let the player move around with style

  • A new time attack mode where you race to beat a pre-determined time

  • A new control remapping menu to change how the game controls to your liking

Spring Escape follows the story of Patchy, a sentient piece of dirt with an all-natural, flourishing hairstyle, and Tulip, an energetic flower ready to jump into action (and conclusions). This duo of nature wake up in a mysterious testing facility on the brink of collapse, but the two are determined to get out of this place, while possibly understanding what got them there in the first place.
